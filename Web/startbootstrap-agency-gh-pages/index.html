Metrics

Here we examine three metrics to assess international spending priorities.

Healthcare

The national spending on heathcare for its residents. Data originally captured as % GDP, but converted into current USD.

Data sourced from the World Bank.

Education

The national spending on education, from primary to tertiary education. Data originally captured as % GDP, but converted into current USD.

Data sourced from the World Bank, and missing values filled in with supplimental data from OECD.

Military

The national spending on military, in current USD.

Data sourced from the World Bank.
